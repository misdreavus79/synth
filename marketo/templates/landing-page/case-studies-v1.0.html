About Change University

Led by Ayelet Hines, Change University hosts suite of online trainings designed specifically for improving campaign skills of progressive nonprofits. She has helped people win campaigns on progressive issues for over 20 years, using her skills in organizing, fundraising, strategic communications, lobbying, and training. Her campaigns include work with the Environmental Defense Fund, Sierra Club, Amnesty International, Center on Alcohol Marketing and Youth, American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees, Democratic National Committee, TexasObserver, National Democratic Institute, Texans Standing Tall, among others.

Ayelet's training experience includes teaching a course at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health, titled Introduction to Campaigning & Organizing.

Find out more about Ayelet and Change University by visiting the Change University website.
