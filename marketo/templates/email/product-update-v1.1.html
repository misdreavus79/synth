Synthesio Password Enhancements

New Features for Securing Your Account

{{lead.First Name:default=Friend}},

I wanted to drop you a quick note to let you know that over the next month or two, we will be rolling out some changes to the way we handle and store user passwords inside of Synthesio. As part of this, your current password will be expired, and you will need to select a new password.

During this process we are going to expire every user’s password, so starting sometime this month when you log in you may be prompted to set a new password.

What action do I need to take?

On this page, simply select a new, valid password (see below for password rules), enter it in both boxes, and then hit Save. That’s it!

What kinds of passwords can I use?

You can use any password that passes the following rules:

  • At least 8 characters long
  • Has at least one of each of the following: 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, 1 number
  • Does not match any of your past three passwords

Why are you making me change my password?

Inside the Synthesio platform, we’ve been busy adding new functionality to enable us to support more complex scenarios for authenticating users onto the platform. These changes require that we reset people’s passwords once. These changes also allow us to support expiring your password on a flexible schedule to conform to your corporate security policies, and in the future, will allow us to support SSO and other advanced authentication methods.

Where can I find more information?

If you have any questions about this process -- or our platform security measures in general -- please reach out to your account manager or myself at mzito@synthesio.com and we’ll be happy to get you the information you need.
