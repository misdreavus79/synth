The security of our customers’ data is our foremost concern, which is why we’re always working with the latest standards and protocols to ensure your data is fully protected.

What is the change?

We are currently adding new features across the platform to secure user passwords and bolster reliability. As part of this process we are going to expire every user’s password, so starting this month when you log in you may be prompted to set a new password.

What action do I need to take?

On this page, simply select a new password, enter it in both boxes, and then hit Save. That’s it!

Why are we making this change?

While this might seem minor, it’s a huge step forward in the security of your data.

Where can I find more information?

If you have any questions about this process -- or our platform security measures in general -- please reach out to your account manager and we’ll be happy to help!
